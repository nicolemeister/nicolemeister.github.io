Child Marriage, Human Trafficking: An Empirical Ecosystem Analysis for Bangladesh

Analyzed international child marriage trends to identify relationship with human trafficking metrics and visualize change in child marriage rates.

Methods to Identify Patient Clusters and Build Precision Analytics for Diagnosis

Americas Committee for Treatment and Research in Multiple Sclerosis (ACTRIMS) , 2021
